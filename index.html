Trail For Humanity

In the name of social justice and human rights, we, the women of AF3IRM, local communities, and allies have pledged to walk over 300 miles. We will be caravanning in the Bay Area July20-21st the LOS ANGELES DETENTION CENTER and caravanning to the TIJUANA BORDER to give voice to the people suffering from injustice around the world. We demand:

  • Protection of immigrant women and their families
  • Comprehensive immigration reform
  • Eradication of racial profiling Policies
